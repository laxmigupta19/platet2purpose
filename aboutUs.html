About Plate2Purpose

Turning Surplus into Support — One Plate at a Time

Who We Are

Plate2Purpose is a web application that aims to reduce food wastage in hostels and ensure that leftover edible food is delivered to nearby NGOs and shelter homes.

Our Mission
  • Track and quantify daily food waste in hostels
  • Partner with NGOs and local shelter houses for food distribution
  • Build awareness about sustainable food habits

With every plate saved, we bring hope to someone in need. Join us in our mission to make a meaningful impact—right from your dining hall!
